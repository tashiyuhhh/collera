About Bell Church

Lion Dance (舞狮)

The Lion Dance is one of the most iconic cultural traditions performed during festivals and grand celebrations. Dancers embody the movements of the lion with precision and energy, accompanied by the sound of drums, cymbals, and gongs. It is believed to chase away evil spirits and bring good fortune.

Dragon Dance (舞龙)

The Dragon Dance is a breathtaking performance symbolizing strength, power, and wisdom. The dragon is brought to life by a team of skilled performers who move in perfect harmony with the rhythmic drumbeats, creating an awe-inspiring spectacle.

Drum Exhibition (鼓展)

The Drum Exhibition highlights the energy and power of traditional Chinese percussion instruments. Each strike echoes discipline, unity, and heritage, energizing both performers and audiences.

Martial Arts (武术)

Martial Arts is not only about combat techniques but also about discipline, respect, and balance between mind and body. Students are trained in strength, flexibility, and endurance while embracing values of humility, perseverance, and honor.
